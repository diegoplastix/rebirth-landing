Frequently Asked Questions

Is a consultation required before treatment? +

Yes — and by design. Every client begins with a private consultation so we can assess your anatomy, discuss your goals, and recommend a protocol that is genuinely right for you. We do not treat without understanding you first.

Are treatments customized for each individual? +

Absolutely. Every face, every body, every set of goals is unique. We customize each treatment plan based on your anatomy, aesthetic preferences, and desired outcomes. There is no one-size-fits-all approach at Rebirth.

How long do appointments typically take? +

Consultations typically take 45-60 minutes. Treatment appointments vary depending on the service—this will be discussed during your consultation and confirmed when scheduling.

Is there downtime after treatments? +

Downtime varies by treatment. Some procedures have minimal to no downtime, while others may involve mild swelling or redness. We'll provide detailed aftercare instructions and realistic expectations during your consultation and after your treatment.

Do you offer telehealth or virtual consultations? +

We primarily conduct in-person consultations so we can properly assess your anatomy and facial structure. However, we're happy to discuss your goals via phone or video initially—just let us know!
